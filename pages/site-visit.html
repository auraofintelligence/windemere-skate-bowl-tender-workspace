Not for construction / exploratory tender workspace

Site visit agenda

A printable agenda and question set for the mandatory site inspection and the first real partnership conversation.

Printable meeting agenda

Purpose: decide whether there is a realistic tender partnership. The tone should be practical, open and no pressure.

Attendees: Luke Hayes / Strange But True, Paolo, Kieran / VFG Skateparks.

Working assumption: mandatory site inspection at Windemere Road Park, 42-46 Windemere Road, Alexandra Hills QLD 4161, at 10:00 am Wednesday 15 July 2026. Confirm attendance before relying on this.

Print friendlyWorking assumptionRSVP to confirm

Questions to ask on site

  • What are VFG's practical ideas for the bowl repairs?
  • Which defects are safety-critical vs desirable improvements?
  • How would VFG approach cracks over 5 mm and height irregularities over 3 mm?
  • What coping repairs are likely?
  • What street elements would broaden use without overbuilding the site?
  • Where could ledges, rails, manual pads or banks sit while respecting existing flow?
  • Is drainage a design issue, maintenance issue, or both?
  • How do the June photos showing standing water change the site assessment?
  • What survey, levels and service information is needed before CAD gets serious?
  • What is VFG comfortable sketching at tender stage?
  • What needs RPEQ or specialist confirmation?
  • What is excluded from Luke's role?
  • What returnables can each party provide?
  • Who owns pricing, construction methodology, WHS, environmental and QA plans?
  • What would make this a no-bid?
  • What would make this a strong bid?

Observations

Condition, cracking, surface levels, coping, water marks, graffiti, debris, access, shelter, fencing, seating, signs and vegetation.

VFG practical ideas

Repair method, rideability, sequencing, feature options, what is realistic at tender stage and what needs specialist confirmation.

Luke translation / CAD tasks

Sketch capture, concept-only drawings, compliance cross-reference, questions register and draft narrative tasks.

Tender questions

Returnables, exclusions, assumptions, risks, no-bid triggers and evidence needed before submission.

Risks

Drainage, certification, BOQ, site access, environmental controls, role blur and tender timing.

Decisions

Proceed, pause or no-bid. Confirm who owns each next action and who must review it.
